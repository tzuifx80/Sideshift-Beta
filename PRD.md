# SideShift MVP product requirements

SideShift is a private perspective game. A user chooses a take, records a private starting stance, and is asked to defend a side they may disagree with. The product message is: **Can you convincingly defend the side you disagree with?**

## MVP flow

1. Anonymous visitor completes a short onboarding with a display name and at least three interests.
2. Preferences personalize a bilingual library of balanced takes.
3. A user selects Classic, SideSwitch, or Blindside.
4. The user records a private pre-debate stance and confidence.
5. The debate runs through opening, rebuttal, pressure question, steelman, and closing rounds.
6. An opponent response is generated from the selected take, assigned side, language, and latest argument.
7. The user records a post-debate stance and understanding level.
8. A bounded judge result shows argument technique, movement, coaching, and a share card.
9. Friend Clash creates a secure, expiring one-response link that works without an account.

## Product principles

- Private by default; no permanent political profile is required.
- Score argument technique, not agreement with a belief.
- Keep takes understandable, non-inflammatory, and defensible from both sides.
- Use structured rounds and curated content before adding public feeds or live matching.
- Mock AI is allowed for development, but it must be labelled and production AI must stay server-side.

## Out of scope

Random matchmaking, live rooms, public messaging, voice, video, payments, public feeds, complex rankings, and famous-person simulations are not part of this MVP.
